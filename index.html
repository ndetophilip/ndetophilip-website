About The Firm

NDETOPHILIP & COMPANY ADVOCATES is committed to excellence, integrity and client-focused legal strategy. Our practice combines modern legal precision with confidentiality and fearless courtroom advocacy.

We serve individuals, landlords, tenants, families and businesses across Kenya, ensuring their rights, assets and liberties are safeguarded with professionalism and unwavering dedication.
